# Dashboard Guide

The dashboard is your home screen, summarizing the weekly performance of all YouTube channels you track. From subscriber changes to competitor analysis, AI-generated summaries to recently published video performance — all critical channel information is accessible here.

---

## Overview Cards

![Dashboard Overview](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ytScope/ytscope-content/main/images/academy/en/dashboard-panel.webp)

At the top of the page, four summary cards display the key metrics for your selected channel. Use the channel selector in the top-right corner to switch between your own channel and any competitor channels you track.

| Card | What It Shows |
|------|--------------|
| **Subscribers** | Net subscriber gain or loss over the past 7 days. A green arrow indicates growth, a red arrow indicates decline. The mini chart inside the card reflects daily subscriber movement. |
| **Views** | Total view count change over the past 7 days. A rising graph signals increasing reach; a declining graph signals decreasing exposure. |
| **Engagement** | The ratio of likes and comments to total views on your videos. The YouTube average is 3–5%. The percentage below shows the week-over-week trend. |
| **Wins** | The number of key metrics (subscribers, views, engagement, video count) where you outperform the competitor average. For example, **1/4** means you lead in only one area; **4/4** means you lead in all four. |

Each card's bottom row shows the percentage change compared to the previous week, providing a quick read on whether the trend is moving in the right direction.

## Creator DNA

![Creator DNA](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ytScope/ytscope-content/main/images/academy/en/dashboard-creator-dna.webp)

Creator DNA analyzes your channel across **5 dimensions** and generates a unique content creator profile. Each dimension is scored from 0 to 100:

| Dimension | Description |
|-----------|------------|
| **Content Type** | Measures content diversity across your channel (viral, evergreen, etc.). |
| **Consistency** | Indicates how stable and regular your publishing schedule is. |
| **Engagement** | Measures how actively your audience interacts with your videos. |
| **Growth** | Evaluates subscriber and view growth velocity. |
| **Loyalty** | Shows how loyal your audience is and their return rate. |

**Score evaluation:**
- **70 and above:** Strong area — you are performing well in this dimension.
- **30 and below:** Critical area (red) — immediate improvement is needed.

The total DNA score is a composite of all five dimensions. Your strengths and weaknesses are highlighted separately.

### Sharing

![Creator DNA Share](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ytScope/ytscope-content/main/images/academy/en/dashboard-creator-dna-share.webp)

Click the **share** icon on the Creator DNA card to share your DNA profile across different platforms:

| Platform | Action |
|----------|--------|
| **Copy Link** | Copies a shareable URL to your clipboard. |
| **X (Twitter)** | Opens a pre-filled post with your DNA profile. |
| **WhatsApp** | Shares via WhatsApp message. |
| **Telegram** | Shares via Telegram message. |
| **LinkedIn** | Shares as a LinkedIn post. |
| **Facebook** | Shares as a Facebook post. |

### AI Analysis

![AI Analysis](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ytScope/ytscope-content/main/images/academy/en/dashboard-ai-analysis.webp)

Click the **"Generate AI Analysis"** button at the bottom of the Creator DNA card. The AI interprets your DNA metrics in detail, including your content creator archetype, strengths, areas for improvement, and actionable recommendations.

**Example AI Analysis:**

> *"The Viral Hunter archetype has a natural advantage in generating view spikes and reaching new audiences. However, relying solely on viral content can cause growth fluctuations. Content Type is your weakest dimension — aim to publish at least one evergreen video this week that can accumulate views over time."*

## Channel Health

![Channel Health](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ytScope/ytscope-content/main/images/academy/en/dashboard-channel-health.webp)

Channel Health provides a single overall score for your channel's current state. **5 key factors** are analyzed:

| Factor | Description |
|--------|------------|
| **Growth** | Your subscriber and view growth rate. |
| **Engagement** | Your like, comment, and share ratios. |
| **Consistency** | Your content publishing regularity. |
| **Momentum** | Your channel's recent performance direction. |
| **Competition** | Your position relative to competitors. |

Each factor is displayed as a horizontal bar chart. The overall score appears in a circular gauge with a letter grade (A, B, C, D, F).

**Score evaluation:**
- **90+:** Excellent — your channel is strong across all areas.
- **50–89:** Needs improvement — some areas could be optimized.
- **Below 50:** Requires attention — immediate action is recommended.

## AI-Powered Insights

![AI Summary](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ytScope/ytscope-content/main/images/academy/en/dashboard-ai-summary.webp)

The AI analyzes your channel's **past 7 days of data** and delivers a comprehensive summary. This includes:

- Your channel's strengths
- Risk areas and items requiring attention
- Specific improvement recommendations

**Example AI Insight:**

> *"Your channel health is currently stable with a C grade, showing a slight positive trend of +0.3%. Your engagement rate at 5.88% is significantly higher than the YouTube average of 3.87%. However, momentum is your weakest area. Consider building more evergreen content to sustain consistent growth rather than relying on viral spikes."*

## Competitors This Week

![Competitors This Week](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ytScope/ytscope-content/main/images/academy/en/dashboard-competitors-week.webp)

Displays the subscriber changes for each competitor channel you track this week.

| Indicator | Meaning |
|-----------|---------|
| **Green arrow + positive value** | Competitor is gaining subscribers. |
| **Red arrow + negative value** | Competitor is losing subscribers. |
| **Dash (—)** | No significant change. |

Competitors are sorted by subscriber count from highest to lowest.

## How Do I Compare?

![How Do I Compare](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ytScope/ytscope-content/main/images/academy/en/dashboard-competitor-comparison.webp)

This section has two main parts:

### Competitor Score

![Competitor Score](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ytScope/ytscope-content/main/images/academy/en/dashboard-competitor-score.webp)

Shows where you stand relative to the competitor average across **4 key metrics**: subscribers, views, engagement, and video count.

For each metric:
- **You:** Your channel's value
- **Avg.:** The average of your tracked competitors
- Gap percentage and whether the gap is closing or widening

**Color codes:**
- **Green:** You are more than 5% ahead of the competitor average.
- **Red:** You are behind the competitor average.

Click the **"Detailed Comparison"** button at the bottom to open the side-by-side channel comparison page.

### Competitors

![Competitors](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ytScope/ytscope-content/main/images/academy/en/dashboard-competitors-list.webp)

All tracked competitors are ranked by subscriber count from highest to lowest. Each competitor displays:

| Info | Description |
|------|------------|
| **Channel name & avatar** | Visual identification of the competitor. |
| **Total subscribers** | Current subscriber count. |
| **Weekly change** | Subscriber movement this week (green: gain, red: loss). |

Click **"View All"** to access the complete competitor list.

## Recent Activity

![Recent Activity](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ytScope/ytscope-content/main/images/academy/en/dashboard-recent-activity.webp)

The bottom section of the dashboard displays recent developments across all tracked channels in four categories:

### New Releases

Lists videos published within the last 48 hours, along with each video's performance **relative to the channel's own average**. Each video shows view count, like ratio, and time since publication.

| Label | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Above Average** | The video is performing at or above the channel's average hourly view rate. It meets or exceeds expected performance. |
| **Normal** | The video is up to 50% below the channel's average. Performance is not low but warrants monitoring. |
| **Below Average** | The video is more than 50% below the channel's average. Title or thumbnail optimization should be considered. |

### Top Performers

Highlights the **best performing** channels across all your tracked channels (including your own) in each category. The fastest growing, most active, and highest engagement channels appear here.

### Needs Attention

Shows alerts related to your channels or competitors. These may include:

- Subscriber loss
- Declining view trends
- Slowing growth
- Competitors pulling ahead

### Opportunities

Surfaces opportunities detected when competitors show moments of weakness. If a competitor's engagement is declining or growth is slowing, this is a window to take action and gain an advantage.
