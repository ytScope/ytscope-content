# Getting Started with YTScope

Welcome to YTScope! This comprehensive guide will walk you through everything you need to know to start analyzing your YouTube channel performance. From creating your account to running your first analysis, we've got you covered.

## Overview

YTScope is a powerful YouTube analytics platform that helps content creators and marketers:

- **Track channel performance** with detailed analytics
- **Monitor growth trends** for subscribers and views
- **Analyze competitors** to stay ahead in your niche
- **Get smart notifications** about important changes

Let's get started!

---

## Step 1: Visit YTScope Homepage

Navigate to [ytscope.com](https://ytscope.com) to begin your journey.

![YTScope Homepage](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ytScope/ytscope-content/main/images/academy/getting-started/homepage.png)

On the homepage, you'll see:

- **"Start Free"** button - Click this to create your account
- **"Watch Demo"** - Preview the platform features
- Key highlights: **30 days free trial**, **No credit card required**, **5,000+ channels tracked**

The dashboard preview shows what you'll get access to:
- Competitor Rankings
- Growth Comparison charts
- Quick Win Opportunities
- Performance metrics

Click **"Start Free"** or **"Get Started"** in the top navigation to proceed.

---

## Step 2: Create Your Account

You'll be directed to the registration page where you can create your YTScope account.

![Registration Page](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ytScope/ytscope-content/main/images/academy/getting-started/register.png)

### Fill in Your Details

1. **Full Name** - Enter your full name
2. **Email** - Use a valid email address (you'll need to verify it)
3. **Password** - Create a strong password
4. **Confirm Password** - Re-enter your password to confirm

Click the **"Register"** button to create your account.

> **Already have an account?** Click "Login here" to sign in instead.

---

## Step 3: Sign In to Your Account

After registration, you can sign in to your account.

![Login Page](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ytScope/ytscope-content/main/images/academy/getting-started/login.png)

Enter your:
- **Email** - The email you registered with
- **Password** - Your account password

Click **"Login"** to access your dashboard.

> **Forgot your password?** Click "Forgot Password" to reset it via email.

---

## Step 4: Welcome to YTScope - Onboarding

After your first login, you'll see the onboarding welcome screen.

![Onboarding Welcome](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ytScope/ytscope-content/main/images/academy/getting-started/onboarding-welcome.png)

This screen introduces the three core features of YTScope:

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Detailed Analytics** | Track your channel performance with comprehensive metrics |
| **Growth Trends** | See subscriber and view trends over time |
| **Smart Notifications** | Stay informed of important changes to your channel |

The onboarding process has 3 steps:
1. **Welcome** - Introduction (current step)
2. **Channels** - Add YouTube channels to track

Click **"Get Started"** to proceed to channel setup.

> You can also click "Skip for now" if you want to explore the dashboard first.

---

## Step 5: Add Your Channels

This is the most important step - adding YouTube channels to track and analyze.

![Add Channels](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ytScope/ytscope-content/main/images/academy/getting-started/onboarding-add-channels.png)

### Important Information

Before adding channels, note these key points:

- **Data collection starts from the moment you add a channel** - YouTube data cannot be retrieved retroactively
- **Analytics will start appearing within the first 48 hours** for new channels
- **Channels already in our system** have analytics available immediately
- **Free plan allows 3 channels** - You can track up to 3 channels on the free plan
- **1 channel change per month** - You can swap out one channel monthly

### Two Ways to Add Channels

#### Option 1: Add Channel by URL

Enter the channel URL or handle directly:
- Full URL: `youtube.com/@channelname`
- Handle: `@channelname`

Click **"Validate"** to verify and add the channel.

#### Option 2: Search Channels

If the channel is already in our database, you can search for it by name.

![Search Channels](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ytScope/ytscope-content/main/images/academy/getting-started/onboarding-search-channels.png)

1. Type the channel name in the search box
2. Browse through the results showing:
   - Channel name
   - Subscriber count
   - Number of videos
3. Click **"+ Add"** next to the channel you want to track

> **Pro Tip:** If a channel is already in our system, analytics will be available immediately!

---

## Step 6: Select and Confirm Channels

After finding your channels, you can select them and configure your tracking preferences.

![Channel Selected](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ytScope/ytscope-content/main/images/academy/getting-started/onboarding-channel-selected.png)

### "This is my own channel" Option

When you select **only one channel**, you'll see a checkbox:

**"This is my own channel"**

Check this box if:
- You own the YouTube channel
- You want to receive owner-specific insights
- You want to enable channel verification features

> **Note:** This option is only available when adding a single channel.

### Channels to Add Section

Your selected channels appear in the "Channels to Add" section. Review your selection and:

- Click **"Add Channels"** to confirm and add them
- Click **X** next to a channel to remove it from your selection

Remember: Free plan allows **up to 3 channels**.

---

## Step 7: Setup Complete!

Congratulations! Your channels are now set up and ready for analysis.

![Onboarding Complete](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ytScope/ytscope-content/main/images/academy/getting-started/onboarding-complete.png)

### What You'll See

- **"You're All Set!"** confirmation message
- **List of added channels** with:
  - Channel avatar and name
  - "My Channel" badge (if you marked it as your own)
  - Channel handle

### Next Steps

From here, you have two options:

1. **"Start Analyzing"** - Go directly to your dashboard and start exploring your analytics
2. **"Add More Channels"** - Add additional channels if you have slots remaining

Click **"Start Analyzing"** to proceed to your dashboard!
