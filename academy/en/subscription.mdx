## What this guide helps you do

The **Subscription** page shows your current plan, usage, renewal or expiration date, and billing actions in one place. This guide helps you interpret the current account information and review the consequences of a plan change before confirming it.

> Plan names, prices, taxes, usage limits, and features can change over time or vary by account. For every transaction, treat the current amount, date, and entitlements displayed on **Subscription** as authoritative.

## Current Plan and Usage

The **Current Plan** card shows the plan name, status, and, when available, its renewal or expiration date. **Usage** shows the number of tracked channels and the displayed plan limit; the interface identifies unlimited usage separately when it applies.

Interpret the information as follows:

- A **Renews** date identifies the next billing point if the current terms continue.
- An **Expires** date identifies how long access remains available after cancellation.
- If usage is close to its limit, decide which channels are still needed before adding another.
- If a value is missing or appears inconsistent, refresh the page before starting a purchase.

Channel usage and the monthly removal allowance are separate measures. Also review **Plan & Usage** in [Settings](/academy/en/settings) for the detailed counters shown to your account.

## Compare plans

The **Monthly** and **Yearly** switch above the plan cards changes the billing period being displayed. When comparing plans:

1. Confirm the selected billing period.
2. Read each card's features, limits, and amount from the current screen.
3. Use the card marked **Current plan** as your baseline.
4. Consider the renewal date and actual usage needs as well as total cost.

**Upgrade**, **Downgrade**, **Switch to Yearly**, and **View Plans** appear only when relevant to the account state. A missing action does not necessarily indicate an error.

## Review an upgrade

If the upgrade flow says the change takes effect immediately, review **Payment Summary** before confirmation:

- **Credit from current plan**,
- **New plan charge**,
- **Due now**,
- **Next billing date**.

These values can reflect proration and your account's billing state. Never substitute an example amount from a guide for the value on the payment screen. Confirm the plan, billing period, amount due, and next date before the final action.

## Downgrades and scheduled changes

The downgrade flow shows when the change takes effect and which access will be lost. Before confirming:

1. Record the effective date.
2. Check whether current channel and feature usage fits the new limits.
3. Compare the on-screen loss list with your team's workflows.
4. If a **scheduled change** is already shown, verify its date and target plan before starting another action.

A downgrade generally follows the date displayed by the interface; the confirmation dialog for your account is the authority for the exact behavior.

## Cancel and reactivate

**Cancel** opens a confirmation dialog. It states that the plan remains active until the displayed date and lists the access that will be lost afterward. You can provide an optional reason, then choose **Cancel Subscription**.

Before cancellation, check:

- The date through which access remains active,
- Features and limits that will be lost,
- Reports or records you need to download,
- Ongoing work that depends on the plan.

If **Reactivate** appears after cancellation is scheduled, it lets you continue the subscription under the terms shown on screen. After the action, verify that the plan status and renewal date update.

## Manage billing and invoices

**Manage Billing** may take you to a secure billing area for actions such as updating a payment method. Verify the destination domain and account. Never enter card details in Academy, AI Copilot, or a support message.

**Billing History** can show:

- Date,
- Description,
- Amount,
- Status: **Paid**, **Pending**, **Failed**, or **Refunded**,
- An invoice download action when available.

If the history is empty, distinguish between a new account, no invoice having been issued, and data still loading. Before retrying a pending or failed payment, refresh the current plan and billing history to confirm its latest state.

## Guided task: Coğrafyanın Kodları

On **July 17, 2026**, perform this check without purchasing or changing a plan:

1. Note the plan status and displayed date in **Current Plan**.
2. In **Usage**, review the tracked channel count that includes **Coğrafyanın Kodları**.
3. Compare that counter with active channels in [Channel Management](/academy/en/channel-management).
4. If you expect to add another channel, evaluate the options using only the current limit and plan cards shown in your account.
5. Leave without selecting a confirmation action; this task verifies capacity rather than making a purchase.

This is a dated example. When you perform it, use the current counters, entitlements, and dates shown in your account.

## Loading, empty, error, and locked states

- **Loading**: Do not start a transaction until the plan and usage details finish loading.
- **Empty billing history**: First confirm loading has completed and you are using the intended account.
- **Payment or action error**: Do not submit the same action repeatedly; refresh the plan state and billing history to verify the result.
- **Locked feature**: Use the current plan comparison on screen. Do not assume a fixed price or entitlement.
- **Date or amount mismatch**: Do not confirm. Contact support with the transaction reference and displayed wording, without sharing payment credentials.
