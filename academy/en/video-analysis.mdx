# Video analysis

The Videos page lists all videos from your tracked channels in one place. Filter, sort, find top performers, and access detailed analytics for any individual video.

<Screenshot
  src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ytScope/ytscope-content/main/images/academy/en/videos-list.webp"
  alt="Videos page list view"
  caption="Videos page — all tracked channel videos in a filterable grid layout."
/>

The page has three tabs:

| Tab | Content |
|-----|---------|
| **List** | Filter, search, and sort all videos. |
| **Top** | Highest-performing videos of the selected period. |
| **Compare** | Compare selected videos side by side. |

---

## List tab

The List tab shows all videos from your tracked channels. The toolbar at the top provides filtering controls.

| Control | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| **Search** | Search for keywords in video titles. |
| **Channel filter** | View videos from a single channel or list all. |
| **Content type** | Filter between **All**, **Regular**, or **Shorts** videos. |
| **Sort** | Sort by date, views, likes, or engagement. |
| **Period** | Select the time range (7 days, 30 days, 90 days, 365 days). |

Each video card shows the thumbnail, title, channel name, view count, likes, and comments. Click a card to open the video detail page.

## Top tab

<Screenshot
  src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ytScope/ytscope-content/main/images/academy/en/videos-top.webp"
  alt="Top performers tab"
  caption="Top — highest-performing videos displayed in a podium layout."
/>

This tab ranks the best videos in the selected period. Sort by four metrics:

| Sort by | Shows |
|---------|-------|
| **Views** | Most-viewed videos. |
| **Likes** | Most-liked videos. |
| **Comments** | Videos with the most comments. |
| **Engagement** | Videos with the highest engagement rate. |

The top 3 videos appear in a podium layout. The full ranking continues below.

<Callout type="tip">
Switch between different metrics. Videos with low views but high engagement may have reached a small but dedicated audience.
</Callout>

## Video detail page

Click any video in the list to open its detail page.

<Screenshot
  src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ytScope/ytscope-content/main/images/academy/en/video-detail-header.webp"
  alt="Video detail page"
  caption="Video detail page — performance score, metrics, and channel ranking."
/>

### Video header

The top section displays the video's basic information:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Thumbnail** | Video preview image and duration. |
| **Title** | Video title. |
| **Publish date** | The date the video was published. |
| **Channel** | Channel name and handle that owns the video. |
| **Watch on YouTube** | Opens the video on YouTube in a new tab. |

### Performance score

The circular indicator in the top-right corner shows the video's overall performance grade. The score ranges from 0 to 100 with a letter grade (A+ through F).

| Sub-score | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **View Performance** | How the video's views compare to the channel average. |
| **Engagement** | Like, comment, and share ratio. |
| **Velocity** | The video's view momentum in its first days. |
| **SEO Score** | Title, description, and tag optimization for search. |

Each sub-score is shown with a colored bar: green for strong, yellow for average, red for weak.

### Overview metrics

Below the performance score, 4 summary cards appear:

| Card | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Views** | Total view count. |
| **Likes** | Total like count. |
| **Comments** | Total comment count. |
| **Engagement** | Engagement rate — percentage of interactions per 100 views. |

If a yellow highlight banner appears, the video ranks among the channel's best in a specific metric (e.g., "This video is among the channel's best in engagement").

### Video vs channel average

Each metric is compared against the channel's overall average. You see what percentage of the channel average the video's views, likes, and comments represent.

### Channel ranking

The video's rank among all videos on the channel is shown. Badges like **"Top 10%"** summarize the video's position. Separate rankings by views, likes, and comments are listed.

<ProTip>
Videos with a low performance score but high engagement sub-score have reached a small but highly interested audience. Carry those topics forward into future content.
</ProTip>
