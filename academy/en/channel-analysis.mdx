# Channel analysis

The channel detail page brings together all performance data for a single channel. From subscriber growth to video performance, daily metrics to AI-powered analysis, examine every aspect of the channel in one place.

## Channel header

The top section displays the channel's identity and summary metrics.

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Avatar and name** | Channel profile image and name. |
| **Rank badge** | The channel's rank among your tracked channels (e.g., **#2**). |
| **Trend percentage** | Growth percentage for the selected period (e.g., **+0.3%**). |
| **Handle and description** | YouTube username and channel description. |
| **Compare button** | Adds the channel directly to the comparison page. |
| **Open on YouTube** | Opens the channel's YouTube page in a new tab. |

### Overview cards

Below the header, 4 summary cards appear. Use the period selector in the top-right corner to change the time range (7, 30, 90, or 365 days).

| Card | Value shown |
|------|-------------|
| **Subscribers** | Current subscriber count and period change percentage. |
| **Views** | Total view count and period change percentage. |
| **Video Count** | The channel's total number of videos. |
| **Recent Videos** | Number of videos published in the selected period. |

<Callout type="tip">
A green arrow and positive percentage indicate growth. When you change the period selector, all cards and sections below update automatically.
</Callout>

---

## Tabs

The channel detail page has 3 tabs:

| Tab | Content |
|-----|---------|
| **Overview** | Growth analysis, performance chart, video stats, and daily table. |
| **Advanced** | Upload time analysis, engagement benchmark, seasonal trends, and more. |
| **Predictions** | Momentum score, churn analysis, and velocity decay forecasts. |

This guide covers the **Overview** tab. For the other tabs, see [Advanced Analytics](/academy/en/advanced-analytics) and [Predictions](/academy/en/predictions).

## Growth analysis

The Growth Analysis section summarizes the channel's subscriber and view gains.

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Positive Days** | Number of days with subscriber gains in the period. |
| **Negative Days** | Number of days with subscriber losses in the period. |
| **Net Change** | Total net subscribers gained or lost. |
| **Views Gained** | Total views gained during the period. |

The status badge at the top indicates the channel's growth health:

| Badge | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Excellent** | Strong and steady growth. |
| **Good** | Positive trend with minor fluctuations. |
| **Attention** | Growth is slowing or volatile. |
| **Critical** | Sustained subscriber loss. |

Below the badge, a bar chart shows daily subscriber changes. Sudden spikes may indicate viral content.

## Performance chart

<Screenshot
  src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ytScope/ytscope-content/main/images/academy/en/channel-overview-mid.webp"
  alt="Performance chart and video performance analysis"
  caption="Performance chart — growth trend, video upload markers, and AI analysis button."
/>

The performance chart visualizes the channel's changes over time. It offers three controls:

| Control | Options |
|---------|---------|
| **Metric** | Subscribers or Views. |
| **Mode** | **Growth** (daily percentage change) or **Absolute** (raw numbers). |
| **Moving average** | Toggles a 7-day moving average line. Smooths out daily fluctuations. |

Orange dots on the chart mark video upload dates. Visually track the relationship between uploads and view spikes.

<Callout type="note">
Click the **"Generate AI Analysis"** button to get automated commentary and recommendations about this chart.
</Callout>

## Video performance analysis

This section displays aggregated stats for videos published in the selected period.

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Avg. Views** | Average view count per video. |
| **Avg. Likes** | Average likes per video. |
| **Avg. Comments** | Average comments per video. |
| **Engagement Rate** | Engagement rate — the number of likes, comments, and shares per 100 views. |

### Content distribution

A horizontal bar shows the split between **Shorts** and **Long-form** videos in the period. Video count and average views are listed separately for each category.

### Top performer

The most-viewed video of the period is highlighted with its title, thumbnail, and view count.

<ProTip>
Compare Shorts and long-form averages. If short videos have higher average views, it may be worth increasing your Shorts output.
</ProTip>

All videos published in the selected period appear as thumbnail cards. Each card shows the video title, views, and likes.

Sort options:

| Sort by | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Views** | Highest to lowest views. |
| **Likes** | Most to fewest likes. |
| **Comments** | Most to fewest comments. |
| **Engagement** | By engagement rate. |
| **Publish date** | Newest to oldest. |

---

## Daily performance table

At the bottom of the page, a table shows the last 14 days of metrics.

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Date** | Day (e.g., Mar 7). |
| **Subscribers** | Total subscriber count for that day. |
| **Sub Change** | Subscriber gain or loss vs. previous day. Green for gains, red for losses. |
| **Views** | Total view count for that day. |
| **View Change** | View gain or loss vs. previous day. |

<Callout type="tip">
Use the daily table to spot sudden spikes in views or subscribers. Check which video was published on days with high view increases.
</Callout>
