This guide covers every step from your first visit to reaching the dashboard. You'll create an account, verify your email, complete onboarding, and add your first YouTube channel.

## Create your account

<Step number={1} title="Open the registration page">
Click the **"Get Started"** button in the top right corner of the homepage. The registration form opens.
</Step>

<Screenshot
  src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ytScope/ytscope-content/main/images/academy/en/gs-register.webp"
  alt="Registration form"
  caption="Registration form — name, email, and password fields."
/>

<Step number={2} title="Fill in the form">
The registration form has four fields:
</Step>

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Full Name** | Enter your real name (minimum 2 characters). |
| **Email** | Enter a valid email address. A verification link will be sent here. |
| **Password** | Must contain at least 8 characters, one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, and one digit. |
| **Confirm Password** | Re-enter your password. Both fields must match. |

<Step number={3} title="Click Register">
Complete the Cloudflare verification and click **"Register"**.
</Step>

<Callout type="tip">
You can also sign up with your Google account. Click **"Continue with Google"** — your account is created without filling in the form.
</Callout>

---

## Email verification

After registration, a verification link is sent to your email address. Check your inbox and click the link.

| Status | What to do |
|--------|------------|
| **Email not received** | Check your spam/junk folder. After 60 seconds, click **"Resend"**. |
| **Link expired** | Request a new verification email from the login page. |
| **Verification successful** | You're redirected to the login page. |

<Callout type="warning">
You can't log in without verifying your email. If you signed up with Google, the verification step is skipped.
</Callout>

## Onboarding: add your first channel

After your first login, a three-step onboarding process begins. You'll add your YouTube channel to the system.

### Step 1 — Welcome

The welcome screen shows YTScope's key features. Click **"Get Started"** to move to the channel setup step.

### Step 2 — Add channels

Add your YouTube channel using one of two methods:

| Method | How |
|--------|-----|
| **URL** | Paste your YouTube channel URL. The system identifies the channel automatically. |
| **Search** | Type the channel name and select it from the list. |

After adding a channel, enable the **"Mark as my own channel"** option. This helps the system distinguish your channel from competitors and deliver personalized analysis.

<Callout type="note">
The Free plan supports **1 channel**, Starter **3**, Pro **10**, and Agency **50 channels**. You can add channels up to your plan limit during onboarding.
</Callout>

### Step 3 — Summary and launch

Review the list of channels you've added. Click **"Start Analyzing"** to be redirected to the dashboard.

<ProTip>
Want to add more channels? Click **"Add More"** to return to the channel selection screen.
</ProTip>

## Plan selection and free trial

When you sign up for YTScope, a **30-day free trial** begins. You get full access to all Pro features during the trial. No credit card required.

<Screenshot
  src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ytScope/ytscope-content/main/images/academy/en/gs-subscription-plans.webp"
  alt="Subscription plans"
  caption="Four plan options: Free, Starter, Pro, and Agency."
/>

| Plan | Price | Channel Limit | Key Features |
|------|-------|--------------|--------------|
| **Free** | $0/mo | 1 channel | Basic metrics, video list. |
| **Starter** | $50/mo | 3 channels | Comparison, MCP access, Shorts analysis. |
| **Pro** | $150/mo | 10 channels | Video comparison, Shorts analysis, advanced analytics. |
| **Agency** | $500/mo | 50 channels | Custom reports, PDF export, all features. |

To change your plan, go to the **Subscription** page from the left menu.

## Settings page

After logging in, click the gear icon in the left menu to open the **Settings** page. It has two sections:

| Section | Contents |
|---------|----------|
| **Profile & Preferences** | Name, email, password change, language selection (English/Turkish), theme (Dark/Light). |
| **Plan & Usage** | Current plan, trial period, tracked channel count, AI credit balance. |
