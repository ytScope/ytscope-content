The dashboard summarizes the performance of all your tracked channels on a single screen. Channel health, competitor status, new videos, and AI-powered insights are all here.

## Channel Health and Creator DNA

The top section of the dashboard contains two main cards: **Channel Health** score on the left and **Creator DNA** profile on the right.

### Channel Health (Health Score)

The Health Score condenses your channel's last 7 days of performance into a single number using a weighted average of five factors. The score ranges from 0 to 100 and receives a letter grade (A+ through F).

| Factor | What it measures |
|--------|-----------------|
| **Growth** | Subscriber growth rate and direction. |
| **Engagement** | Like, comment, and share ratio. |
| **Consistency** | How regularly you publish content. |
| **Momentum** | Direction of growth — accelerating or slowing? |
| **Competitive** | Your position relative to competitors. |

Each factor scores 0-100 and displays as a progress bar. The text below indicates whether you're ahead of or behind the competitor average.

| Score Range | Grade | Meaning |
|------------|-------|---------|
| **80-100** | A+ / A | Your channel shows strong performance. |
| **60-79** | B | Above average — room for improvement exists. |
| **40-59** | C | Average — focus on specific factors. |
| **0-39** | D / F | Critical — immediate action needed. |

<Callout type="tip">
Track your Health Score weekly. A single viral video can temporarily inflate it; a consistent downward trend signals a strategy change is needed.
</Callout>

### Creator DNA

Creator DNA analyzes your content production identity across five dimensions and assigns a "creator type."

| Dimension | What it measures |
|-----------|-----------------|
| **Content Type** | Your content variety and format preference. |
| **Consistency** | Upload frequency and regularity. |
| **Engagement** | Viewer interaction rate. |
| **Growth** | Subscriber acquisition speed. |
| **Loyalty** | Viewer return rate. |

The total score is calculated out of 100. Your creator type badge appears in the top right (e.g., "Viral Hunter," "Community Builder," "Rising Star").

<ProTip>
Use the share button in the top right corner of the Creator DNA card to share your results on social media.
</ProTip>

## KPI cards

Below the Health section, four key metric cards appear. Each card shows the 7-day change and the trend compared to the previous week.

<Screenshot
  src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ytScope/ytscope-content/main/images/academy/en/dashboard-ai-analysis.webp"
  alt="KPI cards and AI summary"
  caption="Four KPI cards: Subscribers, Views, Engagement, and Wins."
/>

| Card | Value Shown | Trend |
|------|-------------|-------|
| **Subscribers** | Net subscribers gained/lost in the last 7 days. | Green arrow = growth, red arrow = loss. |
| **Views** | Total view change over the last 7 days. | Percentage difference vs. previous week. |
| **Engagement** | Engagement rate — likes, comments, and shares per 100 views. | Change compared to previous week. |
| **Wins** | Metrics won against competitor average (e.g., 2/4). | Compares subscribers, views, engagement, and video count. |

<Callout type="note">
The Wins card only appears when you've added competitor channels. Without competitors, this card is hidden.
</Callout>

## AI-powered insights

Below the KPI cards, two AI-powered sections appear: **AI Summary** and **Quick Wins**.

### AI Summary

AI analyzes your channel's last 7 days of data and generates a natural-language summary. It covers your channel's strengths, weak areas, and recommended actions.

The summary is truncated to 3 lines by default. Click **"Read more"** to see the full text. Click **"Generate AI Analysis"** to request a fresh analysis.

### Quick Wins

Quick Wins lists immediately actionable recommendations sorted by importance.

| Type | Example Message |
|------|----------------|
| **Upload needed** | "You haven't uploaded in X days." |
| **Low engagement** | "Your engagement rate is X% below average." |
| **Competitor weakness** | "Competitor [name] is losing subscribers." |

Each recommendation's impact level (High/Medium/Low) is indicated with a colored dot.

## My Channel and competitors

The middle section of the dashboard shows your channel's weekly summary and competitor information.

<Screenshot
  src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ytScope/ytscope-content/main/images/academy/en/dashboard-channel-health.webp"
  alt="My Channel and competitor cards"
  caption="My Channel card, Competitors This Week, and competition comparison section."
/>

### My Channel

The **My Channel** card on the left summarizes your channel's weekly performance:

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Subscriber change** | Net subscribers gained/lost in the last 7 days. |
| **View change** | Total view increase/decrease over the last 7 days. |
| **Video count** | Number of videos uploaded this week. |

Click **"Channel Details"** to go to the channel analysis page.

### Competitors This Week

The card on the right lists your tracked competitors' weekly subscriber changes. Each row shows the channel name, current subscriber count, and this week's change. Click **"All Competitors"** for the full list.

### Competitor Score

The **Competitor Score** card compares your channel against the competitor average across four metrics:

| Metric | Your Value | Competitor Average | Status |
|--------|-----------|-------------------|--------|
| **Subscribers** | Your current subscriber count. | Competitors' average. | Green = ahead, red = behind. |
| **Views** | Your total views. | Competitors' average. | Gap closing/widening shown. |
| **Engagement** | Your engagement rate. | Competitors' average. | Percentage difference noted. |
| **Videos** | Your total video count. | Competitors' average. | Difference shown as a count. |

The badge at the top (e.g., **"2/4 ahead"**) summarizes your overall competitive standing. Click **"Detailed Comparison"** to visit the channel comparison page.

### Competitors list

Next to the Competitor Score, a ranked list shows all competitor channels sorted by subscriber count. Each row displays the rank, channel name, subscriber count, and weekly change trend.

## Recent activity

The bottom section of the dashboard contains three cards: new videos, top performers, and alerts.

<Screenshot
  src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ytScope/ytscope-content/main/images/academy/en/dashboard-my-channel.webp"
  alt="New videos, top performers, and alerts"
  caption="Videos published in the last 48 hours, top performing channels, and opportunities."
/>

### New releases

Lists videos published in the last 48 hours. Each row includes:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Thumbnail and title** | Video preview and full title. |
| **Channel name and time** | Which channel uploaded it and how long ago. |
| **Views** | Current view count and hourly growth rate. |
| **Engagement** | Like-to-view ratio (%). |
| **Performance badge** | "Normal," "Inconsistent," or status vs. channel average. |

<Callout type="tip">
If your own video appears in the list and performs below 50% of your channel average, the system automatically provides a suggestion.
</Callout>

### Top performers

Highlights channels in three categories:

| Category | What it measures |
|----------|-----------------|
| **Fastest Growing** | Channel that gained the most subscribers recently. |
| **Most Active** | Channel that uploaded the most videos in the last 30 days. |
| **Highest Engagement** | Channel with the highest engagement rate. |

### Alerts and opportunities

The **Alerts** section lists warnings that require immediate action:

| Alert Type | Example |
|------------|---------|
| **Subscriber loss** | Subscriber count is dropping. |
| **Views dropping** | Views decreased by X% week-over-week. |
| **Competitor passed** | X competitor channels passed you in subscribers. |

The **Opportunities** section shows competitor weaknesses:

| Opportunity Type | Example |
|-----------------|---------|
| **Competitor losing subs** | "[Channel name] lost X subscribers." |
| **Competitor inactive** | "[Channel name] hasn't uploaded in X days." |
| **Low engagement** | "[Channel name] engagement rate is only X%." |
