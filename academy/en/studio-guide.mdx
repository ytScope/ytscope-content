By the end of this guide, you can move from a competitor signal to a topic, publishing approach, and content draft in Studio.

## Before you begin

- Add a reference channel as **My Channel** and channels you want to benchmark as **Competitors**.
- **Performance Predictor** and personalized suggestions become useful when enough video history is available.
- AI generation tools may use your account's current feature access and credits.
- Generated output is not a published asset. Copy anything you want to keep before leaving the page.

## How to reach this screen

Select **Studio** from the left navigation. The page follows three stages:

| Section | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **Discover** | Inspect competitor movement, outlier videos, and content gaps. |
| **Analyze** | Use your own video history for forecast ranges and topic ideas. |
| **Create** | Draft titles, a script outline, description, SEO suggestions, and a thumbnail brief. |

## Discover: read competitor signals

### What Should You Film Today?

**Last 48 Hours** highlights hourly view velocity for recent competitor videos. **7 Days** highlights videos that look unusual relative to the comparison group. Hourly velocity is a current signal; a Z-score describes distance from the group. Neither guarantees future success.

Selecting a **Content Signal** can send its topic to generation tools. **Topic applied from Pulse** identifies the active context, and **Clear topic** removes it.

### Opportunity Radar

| Tab | How to read it |
|---|---|
| **Breakout** | Videos showing unusual performance signals within the competitor group. |
| **Gaps** | Possible differences between competitor topics and topics covered by your channel. |
| **Format** | Sample count and average views for Shorts and long-form content. |
| **Hidden** | Relatively low-view, high-engagement videos that may signal a small but interested audience. |
| **Activity** | Video and view summaries by competitor. |

Read every average with its sample count. A format with few videos can be heavily influenced by one upload.

## Analyze: use forecasts within their limits

### Performance Predictor

**All**, **Long-form**, **Shorts**, and publish-day filters narrow the sample. **Low**, **Median**, and **High** form a range, not a guaranteed view target.

| Field | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Estimated Views** | Low–median–high range based on similar historical videos. |
| **Best Publish Day** | Strongest day in the current sample; it does not prove causation. |
| **Best Video Length** | Duration range highlighted by historical data. |
| **Title Patterns** | Repeated structures observed in past titles. |
| Confidence label | Summarizes how sample size and distribution support the estimate. |

After changing a filter, check the **based on videos** count again. Do not use a low-confidence or insufficient-data result as a publishing commitment.

### Topic Ideas

**Generate Ideas** uses channel and competitor context. Open **Why?** for each idea and inspect its evidence before choosing it.

## Create: treat output as a pre-publishing draft

| Tool | Output | Final check |
|---|---|---|
| **Title Generator** | Title options and keyword signals | Check promise, accuracy, and channel voice. |
| **Script Outline** | Opening hook, sections, and outro | Verify sources, facts, and flow. |
| **Description Generator** | Description, tags, and hashtags | Update links, dates, and claims. |
| **SEO Optimizer** | Keyword targets, tags, and search intent | Do not treat it as a search-ranking guarantee. |
| **Thumbnail Brief** | Concept, text, color, and emotional direction | Check brand fit, readability, and misleading claims. |

A copy action only writes to your clipboard. It does not publish to YouTube or modify a video.

## Loading, empty, error, and locked states

| State | Next step |
|---|---|
| No competitors added | Add at least one channel as a **Competitor** on **My Channels**. |
| No breakout or gaps | Treat it as a valid empty state; check the active window and refresh later. |
| Insufficient video data | Broaden the filter or wait for more channel history. |
| Analysis failed | Use **Refresh** or the visible retry action. |
| Generation failed | Simplify the topic and retry; avoid repeatedly submitting the same request. |
| Tool locked or credits unavailable | Read the locked card and check **Settings > AI Credits**. |

## Limits and troubleshooting

- A competitor signal does not mean the same topic will perform the same way for your channel.
- Keep low, median, and high forecast scenarios instead of reducing the range to one target.
- Verify names, numbers, sources, and recency in AI output before publishing.
- Studio does not publish to YouTube or take action without your confirmation.
